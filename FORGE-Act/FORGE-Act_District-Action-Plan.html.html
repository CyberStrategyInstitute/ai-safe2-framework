The 4-Phase Local Plan
From Zero to Influence In 8 Weeks
1
Week 1–2: Learn & Share (15 min/day)

Read this 1-pager. Share it on social media. Send it to 3 friends. The goal: make 10 people in your life aware that the FORGE Act exists and what it would mean for your district.

Post to FacebookEmail 3 friendsPrint & share at workBring to church/civic group
2
Week 3–4: Contact Your Representative

Call, email, or visit your congressional office. You don't need to be an expert — you just need to be a constituent who wants this to pass. Staffers track every contact. Volume matters.

Call (most impactful)Email with form letterTown hall commentOffice visit
3
Week 5–6: Build Local Support

Host a house meeting. Bring FORGE to your local business association, school board, veterans group, or civic organization. The more local voices who co-sign this, the stronger the signal to your rep.

House meeting (5+ people)Business assoc. presentationLetter to local paperLocal radio/podcast
4
Week 7–8: Start Building

Find other builders in your district. Start a guild conversation. Apply for the $2,500 Seed Prep Grant at FORGE.gov. You don't need to wait for the full act to pass to build community around it.

Post "Starting a FORGE guild in [city]"Apply for Seed Prep GrantConnect with local makers/devs

Ready-to-Use Scripts
Copy, Personalize, Send. That's It.
📞 Phone Script — Congressional Office (2 min)
"Hi, my name is [Name] and I'm a constituent in [District]. I'm calling to ask Rep. [Name] to co-sponsor the FORGE Act. It would create 28 to 2,873 funded AI opportunities in our district — with zero dollars going to mega-corporations. It's citizen-first and self-sustaining by Year 5. Can I leave my contact information so the rep's office can follow up?"
📧 Email Template — Representative
Subject: "Please Co-Sponsor the FORGE Act — Constituent Request"

"Dear Rep. [Name], I'm a constituent in [City, State]. I'm writing to ask you to co-sponsor the FORGE Act — the Freedom Open-source Research and Growth Engine. At $5B funding, FORGE creates 28 funded AI projects in our district. At $500B, that's 2,873 opportunities — in every county. Zero dollars go to mega-corporations. The act is self-sustaining by Year 5. I believe this represents a real economic opportunity for our district and a genuine national security investment. Thank you for your consideration."
🤝 House Meeting Opening (3 min talk)
"Thanks for coming. I want to talk for 3 minutes about something that could put real money and real tools into our community — without a penny going to Amazon, Google, or Wall Street. It's called the FORGE Act. The short version: it's a citizen-funded AI program that creates between 28 and 2,873 funded projects in our congressional district. Working code wins. Not proposals. Not connections. If you build something useful, you can win a federal contract in 90 days. But it only happens if our rep hears from us. Here's how we all make that call this week..."
